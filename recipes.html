Salads

Fresh red beet salad

Ingredients For 2 portions
Red beets 2 medium size
Bell pepper 1 yellow, shopped in cubes
Parsley leaves 1 bunch, finely chopped
Garlic 1 clove, finely smashed
Lemon juice 3 tablespoons
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °Celsius
  2. Bake the red beets with skin on until they are soft
  3. Take them out of the oven and let it cool
  4. In the mean time, prepare the rest of the ingredients
  5. When the red beets are luke warm, peel the skin off and chop in cubes
  6. Mix all ingredients in a bowl, add salt and pepper and serve with fresh bread

Soups

Carrot soup

Ingredients For 4 portions
Carrots 600 gr, peeled and roughly chopped
Onion 1 medium size, roughly chopped
Cumin powder 1 tablespoon
Broth (vegetable or meat) 1.2 liters
Olive oil 4 tablespoons
  1. Sauté first onions in olive oil and then add carrots
  2. Sauté for 3 more minutes; add cumin powder, salt and pepper
  3. Continue for 2 more minutes and add the broth
  4. Let the broth simmer until the ingredients are soft
  5. Blend the soup using an immersion blender or in a stand blender to achive a creamy consistency
  6. Serve with chili oil and mango balsamico/vinegar

Lunch

Fresh green peas with carrots and potatos

Ingredients For 2 portions
Green peas 250 gr, fresh
Onion 1 small size, finely chopped in cubes
Carrots 2 small, finely chopped in cubes
Potato 1 medium size, finely chopped in cubes
Olive oil 5 tablespoons
  1. Boil fresh green peas in slightly salted water until al-dente and drain out the water
  2. Sauté first onions in olive oil until they are blankadd carrots and potatos
  3. Add carrots and potatos, sauté for 15 mintues
  4. Add green peas at last,sauté for 5 more mintues
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Enjoy on its own or as a side dish

Dinner

Baked aubergines with onions

Aubergines
Ingredients For 2 portions
2 medium size, cut in half lengthwise
Onion 2 medium size, sliced
Tomatos 2 medium size, grated
Bell pepper 1 red color, cut in 4 lengthwise
Garlic 4 cloves, whole
Olive oil 5 tablespoons
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °Celsius
  2. Score the skin of aubergines, brush the flesh with a bit of olive oil, add a bit of salt
  3. Wrap it in aluminium foil and bake until soft
  4. In the mean time, sauté onions and garlic cloves in olive oil
  5. Add grated tomatos and cook for 10 minutes more
  6. Take the aubergines out of the foil and let it cool for 10 minutes
  7. Scoup out some of the flesh*, fill the aubergine boats with onions
  8. Place a clove of garlic on one end and bell pepper piece on top of onions
  9. Bake aubergine boats for 15 minutes more in oven
  10. Enjoy on its own or as a side dish

*Finely chop the flesh, add some olive oil and lemon juice, and taste with salt and pepper

This makes a great salad, side dish or dip.

Deserts

Apple cake with raspberries and crumble topping

Ingredients For 8 portions
Apples 3 big size, chopped in boats
Raspberries 175 gr
Butter 200 gr unsalted, room tempered
Sugar 200 gr, white granule
Eggs 2 big size, room tempered
Flour 200 gr
Baking powder 1.5 teaspoons
Lemon zest and juice Half lemon
For the crumble topping
Flour 50 gr
Sugar 25 gr, white granule
Butter 50 gr unsalted, cold cut in small cubes
Vanilla sugar 1 teaspoon
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °Celsius
  2. Mix apple boats, lemon juice and a tablespoon of sugar in a bowl, and set it aside
  3. Mix butter and sugar using hand mixer until light and fluffy in a large bowl
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time
  5. Combine flour and baking powder, and sift into the wet mixture
  6. Add lemon zest and mix until combined
  7. Have a 24-cm spring form greased on the sides and lined with parchment paper on the bottom
  8. Pour half of the batter into the springform, place half of the apple boats and raspberries
  9. Scape the rest of the batter into the springform, place the remaining fruits on top
  10. For the crumble topping, mix dry ingredients with cold butter using your fingers, it should have a light breadcrumb texture
  11. Spread it on top of the fruits
  12. Bake it in the middle of the oven for ca 1 hour
