How Does It Work?

Register The Online Garden Farmer`s Market and create a profile. Here you can put a listing of items you want sell or buy, present your garden and even add certificates of your products

Now you are ready to search for items in your area, connect with other gardners or customers.

Once you have ordered your items you can choose to do a private transaction or pay securely via The Online Garden Farmer`s Market.

You can arrange with your gardner whether you would like to pick up your items in person or have it delivered to your door for which you can use the delivery support of The Online Garden Farmer`s Market.

About Us

The Online Garden Farmer`s Market was established in 2015 in Oslo by a few hobby gardners to support a green environment and connect people in their community. Since then it has grown to other communities connecting people with locally produced products and contributing to a greener climate.
