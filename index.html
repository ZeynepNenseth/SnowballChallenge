How does The Online Garden Farmer's Market work?


Create a profile

Register at The Online Garden Farmer's Market and create a profile. Here you can buy products, create sell listings, discuss with other Online Garden Farmers on the forum, share recipes, and add certificates on your products.

Create sell listings

Have you already allocated space in your garden or on your plot, and are ready to sell your products? Great! Head over to Buy/Sell and follow the instructions to create a sell listing.

Buy from other local garden farmers

Head over to Buy/Sell to locally buy other people's products. Use the filters to get exactly what you want - where you want it from.

Payment

For every product, The Online Garden Farmer's Market takes 15% of the price. This is so we can maintain our business, and keep all our customers satisfied with our service. The seller recieves the remaining 85% of the price. All purchases happen via our payment system.

Transport

The seller will have different options on transport. These options differ from seller to seller, but most likely you will be able to pick ut up, or have it delivered to your door via The Online Garden Farmer's Market. Check the sell listing for more info!
